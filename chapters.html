Pittsburgh Survey Portfolio 1908

How the Story is Told.

With such a large amount of information, texts and artwork, the question of how to organize and present it for a modern reader in an accessible way becomes very important. The historic books themselves were organized into four monographs and three volumes of individual essays. We were guided by these groupings, but did not wish to strictly adhere to them, because to do so seemed to favor the larger monographs and leave a lot of the essays in a minor light. We organized the material into seven chapters. Click on the Portfolio 1908 bookcover and you will see all seven chapters scroll by.
